**B. Red Flags & Concerns ⚠️**

Your results indicate you might have anemia, specifically microcytic anemia, which means your red blood cells are smaller than usual and don't carry enough oxygen.  The slightly low vitamin B12 level could be contributing to this.  We also need to look into the slightly low calcium level.


**C. Simulated Doctor Advice 👨‍⚕️**
