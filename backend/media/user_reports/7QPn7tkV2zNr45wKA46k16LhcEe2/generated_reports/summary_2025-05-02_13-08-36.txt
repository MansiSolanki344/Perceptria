**B. Red Flags & Concerns ⚠️**

Geetaben, your lab results show some signs of anemia, which is a condition where your blood doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells to carry adequate oxygen throughout your body. This can cause fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath. The low MCV and MCH suggest microcytic anemia, often linked to iron deficiency or B12 deficiency.  Your slightly low B12 levels support this. The slightly low calcium also warrants attention.  Let's address these together.  It's important to understand that these are manageable conditions.
