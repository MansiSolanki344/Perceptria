**B. Red Flags & Concerns ⚠️**

Your lab results indicate you may have anemia, possibly microcytic anemia, meaning your red blood cells are smaller than usual and may not be carrying enough oxygen.  The slightly low calcium and low Vitamin B12 levels also require attention. Anemia can cause fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath.  The low B12 can contribute to fatigue and neurological symptoms.  Let's work on addressing these concerns.
