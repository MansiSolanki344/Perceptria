**B. Red Flags & Concerns ⚠️**

Your lab results indicate you may have microcytic anemia, characterized by small red blood cells with less hemoglobin. This can cause fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath.  The slightly low calcium and significantly low Vitamin B12 levels also need attention.  These deficiencies can contribute to anemia and other health issues.
