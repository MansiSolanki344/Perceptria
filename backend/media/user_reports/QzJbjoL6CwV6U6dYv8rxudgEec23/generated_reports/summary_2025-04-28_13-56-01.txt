**B. Red Flags & Concerns ⚠️**

Your lab results show you might have anemia, which means your blood has fewer red blood cells than normal.  This can lead to fatigue, weakness, and shortness of breath.  The type of anemia might be microcytic, meaning your red blood cells are smaller than usual. Your slightly low calcium levels and borderline Vitamin B12 deficiency could also contribute to this.  It's important to get this checked thoroughly. Let's address these together. 💖

**C. Simulated Doctor Advice 👨‍⚕️**
